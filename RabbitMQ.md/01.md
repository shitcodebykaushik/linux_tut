# RabbitMQ
- It is open-source message broker that implements the AMQP protocol . (Advances Message Queuing Protocol) and supports other protocols/plugins .It receives,store,routes and delivers messages between producers and consumers,decoupling service and smoothing spikes .

- Inputs : producers publish message (binary/text) to the broker .
- Outputs : consumers receive messages from queuses .
- Gurantees : configurable (at-most-once,at-most-once) via acknoledgements persistence and Qos settings .
